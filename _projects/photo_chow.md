Chow Hang-tung, the former vice-chairperson of the group that organised Hong Kong’s Tiananmen vigils, has been in detention since September 2021 for allegedly inciting subversion under the national security law. She has been awarded a human rights prize by the foreign ministries of France and Germany.

On June 8, 2023, the public finally get to catch a glimpse of the activist when she was being escorted to Court of Final Appeal.
