Spike for Jiang Zemin

Pro-Beijing legislators bowed on stage marks in front of the image of the late former China leader.

The spike and right-angle bows seen in Hong Kong’s legislature.

On December 6, 2022, the seventh day memorial service for former Chinese President Jiang Zemin, legislators together watched over an hour of the memorial ceremony in the Legislative Council Hall. To show their gratitude and respect, the legislators bowed three times in front of the image of the former President.
